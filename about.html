I'm always taking photos.
Living in New York City there's an endless stream of places, people and things to photograph.

Born in the Bronx, I grew up Photographing all things New York.
The first photos I ever took were using my grandma's old camera.

One of my Happiest memories was getting my very own camera on my 14th birthday. Since that day, I have been capturing beauty, strength & character in countless photos. I love to photograph regular people going about their daily lives. That always reveal an unexpected aspect of themselves.

I'm based in New York City but travel for projects all around the globe. As well as a passion for photography, I'm also an avid cycling enthusiast and connoisseur of all things paranormal and supernatural. One of my greatest wishes would be to someday photograph a portrait of Big Foot.
